Welcome to Anglian Views

“I love living in the Anglia Area. I was born brought up and work in Norfolk and Suffolk, I love the wildness of the coastal Marshes, the shingle banks and the long stretches of sand. From Hunstanton in the north of the region to the mud flats around the Deben and beyond in Suffolk, there are countless places to visit and photograph. Even leaving the coast and taking in the rolling countryside there is always something to look in wonder at, the wildlife at any time of the year is wondrous, even in the depths of winter there is a beauty to behold. As this website develops I want to illustrate the region and allow all visitors to look at my view of this part of Britain, sharing my vision through the pages of this site, and letting everyone share through the cards and pictures for sale here. If you have never visited please look through the site, and be tempted to look for yourself, you will not be disappointed!!!!! Rob Steward “
